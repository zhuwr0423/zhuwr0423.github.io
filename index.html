Welcome to my homepage!

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Statistics at UC Irvine . I obtained my Ph.D. in Statistics from the University of Chicago, advised by Professors Rina Foygel Barber and Wei Biao Wu.

I am generally interested in statistical inference problems, with my research situated at the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and stochastic optimization. My recent work focuses on theory and methods of uncertainty quantification for online learning and stochastic optimization. Additionally, I am working on goodness-of-fit testing and predictive inference.
